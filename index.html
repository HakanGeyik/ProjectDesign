Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for ant meal. This classic meal combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with choice of cheese, vegetables or meats.

Preparation Time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation:5 minutes
  • Cooking5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs
